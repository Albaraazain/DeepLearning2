Exam Question 9: The Perceptron Model and Its Limitations
Discuss the perceptron model as originally proposed by Rosenblatt. In your answer, include the following points:

Describe the architecture of the perceptron, including the role of inputs, weights, bias, and the activation (step) function.

Explain the perceptron learning rule, focusing on how the error is calculated and why the update for each weight is scaled by the corresponding input.

Identify and discuss the limitations of the perceptron model, particularly regarding its inability to learn non-linearly separable functions and the challenges posed by the non-differentiability of the step function.
