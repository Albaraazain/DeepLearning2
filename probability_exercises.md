# Probability Exercises

## Experimental Analysis
1. Conduct experiments with varying parameters:
   - Vary m (number of groups, default=500)
   - Vary n (samples per group, default=10)
   - Observe and analyze the results

## Probability Theory
1. Given two events with probability P(A) and P(B):
   - Compute upper bounds on P(A ∪ B)
   - Compute lower bounds on P(A ∪ B)
   - Compute bounds on P(A ∩ B)
   - Use Venn Diagrams to visualize the scenarios

## Distribution and Variance
1. For softmax(o):
   - Compute the second derivative of the cross-entropy loss l(y, ŷ)
   - Compute the variance of the distribution
   - Show it matches theoretical predictions
