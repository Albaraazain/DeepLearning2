Exam Question 2: Batch Size, Learning Rate, and Their Interplay
Analyze the relationship between batch size and learning rate in the context of mini-batch gradient descent. Your answer should include:

Definitions of an iteration and an epoch, and how batch size determines the number of iterations per epoch.

A discussion on how changing the batch size affects the noise in the gradient estimates and the overall convergence behavior of the optimizer.

An explanation of how learning rate scheduling (including warmup phases, step decay, exponential decay, or cosine scheduling) can be used to adjust for different batch sizes and improve convergence.
