Exam Question 6: Interaction Between Batch Size, Learning Rate, and Convergence
Discuss how batch size and learning rate interact to influence the convergence behavior of deep neural networks during training. Your answer should cover:

The definitions of an iteration and an epoch in the context of mini-batch gradient descent, and how batch size determines the number of iterations per epoch.

The impact of a large versus a small batch size on the noise in gradient estimates, including how noise can help or hinder escaping local minima.

Strategies for tuning the learning rate in relation to the chosen batch size, and the implications for converging to wide versus sharp minima in the loss landscape.
