Exam Question 3: Loss Surface Characteristics and the Impact of Batch Size
Deep learning loss surfaces are typically non-convex. Explain the differences between sharp and wide minima in such loss surfaces. Your answer should address:

How sharp minima and wide minima differ in terms of their geometry and the sensitivity to changes in parameters.

The potential impact of batch size on converging to sharp versus wide minima (e.g., the role of gradient noise in escaping narrow local minima).

The implications these types of minima have on the generalization performance of a model on unseen data.
