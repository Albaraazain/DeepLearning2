Exam Question 2: Loss Functions for Classification – Hinge Loss vs. Cross Entropy
Compare and contrast hinge loss and cross entropy loss as used for multi-class classification problems. Your answer should include:

A discussion of the behavior of hinge loss (e.g., satisfaction of a margin, zero gradients when the margin is met) and its implications for training.

An explanation of how cross entropy loss is derived from information entropy and why it provides useful gradients even when the model predictions are close to the correct labels.

The practical trade-offs between these loss functions, including issues of margin setting and numerical stability.
