Exam Question 5: Relationship Between Cross Entropy, KL Divergence, and Maximum Likelihood
Explain how cross entropy loss is related to the Kullback-Leibler (KL) divergence and maximum likelihood estimation. Your answer should include:

A derivation or explanation showing that minimizing cross entropy is equivalent to maximizing the likelihood of the correct labels under the model’s predicted probability distribution.

A discussion on how KL divergence can be interpreted as a measure of distance between two probability distributions and why it is asymmetric.

Implications for model training when the predicted distribution deviates from the true distribution.
