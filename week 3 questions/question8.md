Exam Question 2: Analysis of Hinge Loss Derivatives in Classification
In the context of training a classifier with hinge loss, explain the derivation of the gradient for a weight associated with an input feature when:

The weight connects to a neuron corresponding to an incorrect class (i.e., when the hinge loss term is positive).

The weight connects to a neuron corresponding to the correct class, considering that its contribution appears in all hinge loss terms from the other classes.

Include in your answer the role of the indicator function (or a piecewise definition) in handling the two cases and how it affects the weight update.
