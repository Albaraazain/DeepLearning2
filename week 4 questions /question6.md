Exam Question 6: Softmax and Temperature Scaling in Multi-Class Classification
The softmax function is essential for converting raw model outputs into probabilities in multi-class settings. In your answer, be sure to:

Describe the mathematical formulation of the softmax function and explain how it converts logits into a probability distribution.

Explain the concept of numerical stability in the computation of softmax (for example, by subtracting the maximum logit) and why this is necessary.

Discuss the role of temperature scaling (or softmax with temperature), including how varying the temperature parameter affects the “peakedness” of the probability distribution and its practical implications during training and inference.
