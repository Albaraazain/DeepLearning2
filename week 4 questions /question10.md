Exam Question 4: Non-Convex Optimization and Gradient Descent Methods
Deep neural networks are trained via optimization over non-convex loss surfaces. Discuss the challenges that non-convexity introduces during training and explain how gradient descent methods are applied in such settings. Your answer should include:

A description of the difference between convex and non-convex functions, with examples relevant to deep learning.

An explanation of why non-convex loss surfaces may contain multiple local minima (sharp versus wide minima) and the implications for model generalization.
